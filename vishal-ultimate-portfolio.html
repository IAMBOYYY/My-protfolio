// My Journey

How I got here.

2022 — The Beginning
First Lines of Code
Started with pure HTML and CSS. Built my first static webpage. Got completely hooked on making things appear on screen.
2023 — First Real Products
WAVECUT & AudioRip
Shipped WAVECUT — a browser-based media converter using FFmpeg WebAssembly. Solved complex COOP/COEP constraints. My first "this is actually hard" moment.
2024 — Going Full AI
Firefox Extension, VOICEFORGE, FakeOut
Submitted a voice AI browser extension to Mozilla's addons store. Built VOICEFORGE with ElevenLabs. Created FakeOut — a gamified AI video detection app. Discovered the AI frontier and went all-in.
2025 — Product Streak
SplatWeb, MingleHub, BuildAI, OSINT.EYE
Shipped 4+ major products in rapid succession. Each one harder and better than the last. Started taking on real clients.
2026 — Now
Building for YC
In 12th grade, building products, taking on clients, and aiming for Y Combinator. Every project is a step forward. Still from the Oppo A15s. Still shipping.
Philosophy
Ship first. Perfect later.
A live product with 80% polish beats a perfect product that never ships. I optimize for speed and real feedback over theoretical perfection.
Constraint
Constraints are features.
Building from a phone forced solutions that laptop developers never discover. Limitations sharpen thinking and breed innovation.
End Goal
Y Combinator.
I'm not building side projects. I'm building toward YC. Every product needs to solve a real problem for real people. Geography is irrelevant.
